M2T2L8 — Operaciones sobre RDD

Objetivos

  • Revisar transformaciones comunes: map, filter, flatMap, reduceByKey, groupByKey.

Ejemplo

from pyspark import SparkContext
sc = SparkContext()
rdd = sc.parallelize(['a b a', 'b c'])
words = rdd.flatMap(lambda r: r.split())
counts = words.map(lambda w: (w,1)).reduceByKey(lambda a,b: a+b).collect()
print(counts)
sc.stop()
